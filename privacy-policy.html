VitalBoost Gizlilik Politikasi

Son guncelleme: 24 Mart 2026

Ozet: VitalBoost bir saglik ve yasam asistani uygulamasidir. Tum kisisel verileriniz cihazinizda sifrelenerek saklanir. Fotograflariniz yalnizca AI analizi icin gecici olarak islenir ve sunucularimizda depolanmaz. Uygulama tibbi bir cihaz degildir.

1. Uygulama Hakkinda

VitalBoost asagidaki ozellikleri sunan kapsamli bir saglik ve yasam asistanidir:

  • Urun Tarama: Kamera ile urun kutulari, takviyeler ve vitaminlerin iceriklerini analiz etme
  • Urun Arama: Urunleri isimle arama
  • Barkod Tarama: Barkod okuyarak urun bilgisi alma
  • Urun Dolabi: Taranan urunleri kaydetme ve yonetme
  • Egzersiz Planlama: Yapay zeka destekli kisisellestirilmis 7 gunluk egzersiz programlari
  • Beslenme Programi: Yapay zeka destekli kisisellestirilmis 7 gunluk ogun planlari
  • Yemek Tarifleri: Yapay zeka ile detayli tarif olusturma
  • Hatirlaticilar: Takviye, egzersiz ve ogun hatirlaticlari
  • Tarama Gecmisi: Onceki tarama ve arama sonuclarini goruntuleme

2. Toplanan Veriler

VitalBoost asagidaki verileri toplar ve isler:

  • Kamera goruntuleri: Urun kutusu, etiket ve barkod taramasi icin kamera erisimi kullanilir. Fotograflar yalnizca anlik AI analizi icin gonderilir ve hicbir sunucuda depolanmaz.
  • Galeri fotograflari: Kullanicinin sectigi fotograflar analiz icin kullanilir. Ayni sekilde sunucuda depolanmaz.
  • Fiziksel profil: Boy, kilo, yas, cinsiyet ve fitness seviyesi bilgileri — egzersiz ve beslenme planlari olusturmak icin kullanilir. Bu veriler yalnizca cihazinizda sifrelenerek saklanir.
  • Beslenme tercihleri: Diyet tercihleri, alerjiler ve bolgesel mutfak secimi — beslenme planlari icin kullanilir. Cihazinizda sifrelenerek saklanir.
  • Egzersiz planlari: Yapay zeka tarafindan olusturulan kisisel egzersiz programlari. Cihazinizda sifrelenerek saklanir.
  • Beslenme planlari: Yapay zeka tarafindan olusturulan kisisel ogun planlari. Cihazinizda sifrelenerek saklanir.
  • Urun dolabi verileri: Kaydettiginiz urun bilgileri cihazinizda sifrelenerek saklanir.
  • Hatirlatici verileri: Takviye, egzersiz ve ogun hatirlaticilari cihazinizda sifrelenerek saklanir.
  • Tarama gecmisi: Onceki tarama ve arama sonuclariniz cihazinizda saklanir.
  • Cihaz kimlik numarasi (Device ID): Istek sinirlama (rate limiting) ve abonelik yonetimi icin kullanilir. Kisisel kimliginize bagli degildir.

3. Yapay Zeka (AI) Kullanimi

VitalBoost, urun analizi, egzersiz planlama, beslenme programi olusturma ve tarif uretme icin yapay zeka hizmetlerini kullanir:

  • Google Gemini API (birincil): Ana yapay zeka hizmeti olarak kullanilir.
  • Mistral API (yedek): Birincil hizmetin kullanilamadigi durumlarda devreye girer.
  • OpenRouter API (yedek): Ek yedek hizmet olarak kullanilir.
  • Groq API (yedek): Ek yedek hizmet olarak kullanilir.

Bu hizmetlerle ilgili onemli bilgiler:

  • Fotograflariniz ve profil bilgileriniz analiz icin API'lere gonderilir ancak bu veriler yalnizca anlik islem icin kullanilir.
  • Gonderilen veriler API saglayicilari tarafindan kalici olarak depolanmaz ve model egitimi icin kullanilmaz.
  • Tum API iletisimi sifreli (HTTPS) baglanti uzerinden gerceklesir.

4. Veri Depolama ve Guvenlik

  • Tum kullanici verileri (fiziksel profil, egzersiz planlari, beslenme planlari, urun dolabi, hatirlaticilar, gecmis, ayarlar) yalnizca cihazinizda sifrelenerek yerel olarak saklanir.
  • Sunucularimizda hicbir kisisel veri depolanmaz.
  • Uygulamayi sildiginizde tum yerel veriler kalici olarak silinir.
  • Verileriniz ucuncu taraflarla paylasilmaz ve satilmaz.

5. Kamera ve Fotograf Erisimi

Kamera ve fotograf galerisi erisimi yalnizca asagidaki amaclarla kullanilir:

  • Urun kutusu fotografi cekme veya secme
  • Etiket fotografi cekme veya secme
  • Barkod tarama

Bu izinler istege baglidir ve istediginiz zaman cihaz ayarlarindan kapatabilirsiniz. Kamera ve galeri erisimi olmadan da urun arama ve diger ozellikler kullanilabilir.

6. Bildirim Erisimi

VitalBoost, hatirlatici ozelliklerini kullanabilmek icin bildirim izni talep eder:

  • Takviye hatirlaticilari
  • Egzersiz hatirlaticilari
  • Ogun hatirlaticilari

Bu izin istege baglidir. Bildirim izni vermeden de uygulamanin diger ozelliklerini kullanabilirsiniz.

VitalBoost Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 24, 2026

Summary: VitalBoost is a health and wellness assistant app. All your personal data is stored encrypted on your device. Your photos are processed only temporarily for AI analysis and are not stored on our servers. The app is NOT a medical device.

1. About the App

VitalBoost is a comprehensive health and wellness assistant offering the following features:

  • Product Scanning: Analyze product boxes, supplements, and vitamins using your camera
  • Product Search: Search for products by name
  • Barcode Scanning: Scan barcodes to get product information
  • Product Cabinet: Save and manage scanned products
  • Exercise Planning: AI-powered personalized 7-day exercise programs
  • Nutrition Programming: AI-powered personalized 7-day meal plans
  • Meal Recipes: AI-generated detailed recipes
  • Reminders: Supplement, exercise, and meal reminders
  • Scan History: View previous scan and search results

2. Data We Collect

VitalBoost collects and processes the following data:

  • Camera images: Camera access is used for scanning product boxes, labels, and barcodes. Photos are sent only for instant AI analysis and are not stored on any server.
  • Gallery photos: User-selected photos are used for analysis. They are likewise not stored on any server.
  • Physical profile: Height, weight, age, gender, and fitness level — used to generate exercise and nutrition plans. This data is stored only on your device with encryption.
  • Dietary preferences: Diet preferences, allergies, and regional cuisine selection — used for nutrition plans. Stored encrypted on your device.
  • Exercise plans: Personalized exercise programs generated by AI. Stored encrypted on your device.
  • Nutrition plans: Personalized meal plans generated by AI. Stored encrypted on your device.
  • Product cabinet data: Saved product information stored encrypted on your device.
  • Reminder data: Supplement, exercise, and meal reminders stored encrypted on your device.
  • Scan history: Previous scan and search results stored on your device.
  • Device ID: Used for rate limiting and subscription management. Not linked to your personal identity.

3. Use of Artificial Intelligence (AI)

VitalBoost uses AI services for product analysis, exercise planning, nutrition programming, and recipe generation:

  • Google Gemini API (primary): Used as the main AI service.
  • Mistral API (fallback): Activated when the primary service is unavailable.
  • OpenRouter API (fallback): Used as an additional backup service.
  • Groq API (fallback): Used as an additional backup service.

Important information about these services:

  • Your photos and profile information are sent to APIs for analysis but are used only for instant processing.
  • Sent data is not permanently stored by API providers and is not used for model training.
  • All API communication occurs over encrypted (HTTPS) connections.

4. Data Storage and Security

  • All user data (physical profile, exercise plans, nutrition plans, product cabinet, reminders, history, settings) is stored only locally on your device with encryption.
  • No personal data is stored on our servers.
  • When you uninstall the app, all local data is permanently deleted.
  • Your data is not shared with or sold to third parties.

5. Camera and Photo Access

Camera and photo gallery access is used solely for the following purposes:

  • Taking or selecting photos of product boxes
  • Taking or selecting photos of labels
  • Scanning barcodes

These permissions are optional and can be disabled at any time from your device settings. Product search and other features can be used without camera or gallery access.

6. Notification Access

VitalBoost requests notification permission to enable reminder features:

  • Supplement reminders
  • Exercise reminders
  • Meal reminders

This permission is optional. You can use the app's other features without granting notification access.

7. Third-Party Services

  • Google Gemini API: Used for product analysis, exercise and nutrition planning. Google Privacy Policy
  • Mistral API: Fallback AI service. Mistral Privacy Policy
  • OpenRouter API: Fallback AI service. OpenRouter Privacy Policy
  • Groq API: Fallback AI service. Groq Privacy Policy
  • Expo: App update infrastructure. Expo Privacy Policy

8. Children's Privacy

VitalBoost is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13.

9. Disclaimer

Important: VitalBoost is NOT a medical device or service. Product analyses, exercise programs, nutrition plans, and meal recipes provided by the app are offered for informational purposes only. This information does not replace professional medical, nutritional, or fitness advice. Always consult a healthcare professional or doctor before making decisions about your health.

10. Changes

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. Significant changes will be communicated through the app.
